Hadithi Yetu

Our Story

Jinsi tulivyoanzisha na kukua kuwa wakala wakuu wa usambazaji wa vyakula Tanzania

How we started and grew to become Tanzania's leading food delivery agent

2020

Mwanzilishi

Foundation

Tulianzisha Chakula Haraka na wauzaji 5 wa kwanza huko Kariakoo, Dar es Salaam. Lengo letu lilikuwa kuleta nyama choma kwa wateja haraka.

We founded Chakula Haraka with 5 initial vendors in Kariakoo, Dar es Salaam. Our goal was to deliver nyama choma to customers quickly.

2021

Upanaaji

Expansion

Tuliongeza huduma kufikia maeneo 10 ya Dar es Salaam na wauzaji 50+. Tukaanza kutoa usafirishaji wa bure kwa maagizo makubwa.

We expanded service to 10 areas of Dar es Salaam with 50+ vendors. We started offering free delivery for large orders.

2022

Teknolojia

Technology

Tulizindua tovuti yetu na mfumo wa WhatsApp. Wakati huu tulikuwa na wauzaji 100+ na wateja 10,000+.

We launched our website and WhatsApp system. By this time we had 100+ vendors and 10,000+ customers.

2023

Udhibiti wa Ubora

Quality Control

Tulianzisha mfumo wa uthibitishaji wa wauzaji na udhibiti wa ubora. Wakati huu tulikuwa na wauzaji 150+.

We launched vendor verification system and quality control. By this time we had 150+ vendors.

2024

30-Minute Guarantee

30-Minute Guarantee

Tulianzisha hakikishi ya dakika 30. Chakula hakijafika ndani ya dakika 30, ni bure! Tulifika wateja 50,000+.

We launched 30-minute guarantee. If food doesn't arrive within 30 minutes, it's free! We reached 50,000+ customers.
