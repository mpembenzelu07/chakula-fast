Our Mission to Transform Tanzania's Food Delivery

Tulianzishwa mwaka 2020 kwa dhamira rahisi lakini yenye nguvu: kuleta vyakula vya mitaani vya Tanzania, vilivyo na ladha asilia na usafi wa juu, hadi kwenye meza ya wateja wetu. Leo, tumegeuka kuwa muungano mkubwa wa wauzaji na huduma ya haraka zaidi nchini.

We were founded in 2020 with a simple yet powerful mission: to bring authentic Tanzanian street food, with premium hygiene and quality, to our customers' tables. Today, we've grown into Tanzania's largest vendor network and fastest delivery service.

Kwa kutumia teknolojia ya kisasa na mifumo ya jadi ya upishi, tunahakikisha kuwa kila kisupe kinahifadhi ladha ya asili ya Tanzania wakati huo huo tukiwahi mahitaji ya kisasa ya usafi na haraka.

By combining modern technology with traditional cooking systems, we ensure every dish preserves authentic Tanzanian flavor while meeting modern hygiene and speed requirements.

Our Journey

Kutoka kwa wauzaji 5 wa kwanza hadi muungano mkubwa wa Tanzania

From our first 5 vendors to Tanzania's largest food network

2020

Mwanzilishi

Foundation

Tulianzisha na wauzaji 5 wa kwanza huko Kariakoo. Lengo letu lilikuwa kuleta nyama choma ya darasa la juu kwa wateja haraka na kwa usafi.

We started with 5 initial vendors in Kariakoo. Our goal was to deliver premium nyama choma quickly and hygienically to customers.

Wauzaji 5 wa Kwanza First 5 Vendors
2021

Upanaaji

Expansion

Tulipanua huduma kufikia maeneo 10 ya Dar es Salaam na wauzaji 50+. Tukaanza kutoa usafirishaji wa bure kwa maagizo makubwa na kuanza mifumo ya udhibiti wa ubora.

We expanded service to 10 areas of Dar es Salaam with 50+ vendors. We began offering free delivery for large orders and started quality control systems.

Maeneo 10 10 Areas Covered
2022

Teknolojia

Technology

Tulizindua tovuti yetu na mfumo wa ombi la WhatsApp. Wakati huu tulikuwa na wauzaji 100+ na wateja 10,000+. Tukaanza mfumo wa ufuatiliaji wa maagizo.

We launched our website and WhatsApp ordering system. By this time we had 100+ vendors and 10,000+ customers. We began our order tracking system.

Mfumo wa WhatsApp WhatsApp System
2023

Udhibiti wa Ubora

Quality Control

Tulianzisha mfumo mkali wa uthibitishaji wa wauzaji. Kila muuzaji sasa anapita ukaguzi wa hatua 6 kabla ya kupata cheti cha "Muuzaji Mwenye Thibitisho".

We launched our rigorous vendor verification system. Every vendor now passes a 6-step inspection before receiving our "Verified Vendor" certificate.

Dhamira, Maono & Maadili

Mission, Vision & Values

Kanuni zinazotuongoza katika kubadilisha sekta ya usambazaji wa chakula

Principles guiding our transformation of the food delivery industry

Dhamira Yetu

Our Mission

Kuwaunganisha wateja na wauzaji wa vyakula vya mitaani vya Tanzania kupitia teknolojia, tukihakikisha usafi, ubora, na haraka katika kila kisupe.

To connect customers with Tanzania's street food vendors through technology, ensuring hygiene, quality, and speed in every dish.

Maono Yetu

Our Vision

Kuwa muungano wakuu wa vyakula vya mitaani barani Afrika kufikia 2030, tukihudumia mamilioni ya wateja na kusaidia maelfu ya wauzaji.

To become Africa's leading street food network by 2030, serving millions of customers and supporting thousands of vendors.

Ubora Bora

Premium Quality

Tunatumia viungo vya hali ya juu na kufuata viwango vya usafi vya kimataifa katika kila kisupe.

We use premium ingredients and follow international hygiene standards in every dish.

Haraka ya Kipekee

Unmatched Speed

Dakika 30 guarantee - chakula hakijafika ndani ya dakika 30, ni bure. Tuko haraka zaidi nchini.

30-minute guarantee - if food doesn't arrive within 30 minutes, it's free. We're the fastest nationwide.

Kujali Jamii

Community Care

Tunasaidia wauzaji wetu kukuza biashara zao na kutoa fursa za ajira katika jamii.

We help our vendors grow their businesses and provide employment opportunities in communities.

Uaminifu

Trustworthiness

Kila muuzaji anapita ukaguzi mkali. Hakuna malipo kabla ya kufurahia chakula.

Every vendor passes rigorous inspection. No payment before enjoying your food.

Uendelevu

Sustainability

Tunatumia vifaa vinavyoweza kutumiwa tena na kusaidia wauzaji wetu kutumia rasilimali zenye uendelevu.

We use reusable packaging and help our vendors use sustainable resources.

Usawa

Inclusion

Tunatoa fursa sawa kwa wauzaji wote, haijalishi ukubwa wa biashara yao.

We provide equal opportunities to all vendors, regardless of their business size.
