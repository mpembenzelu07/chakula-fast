Frequently Asked Questions

Majibu kwa maswali yote muhimu kuhusu huduma yetu

Answers to all important questions about our service

Je, gharama ya usafirishaji ni ngapi? How much is delivery cost?

Usafirishaji ni BURE kabisa kwa maagizo yote ya TSh 10,000 na zaidi. Kwa maagizo chini ya TSh 10,000, ada ndogo ya TSh 1,500 inatumika.

Delivery is completely FREE for all orders of TSh 10,000 and above. For orders below TSh 10,000, a small fee of TSh 1,500 applies.

BONUS: Usafirishaji bure daima kwa maagizo ya asubuhi (6am-10am)

BONUS: Always free delivery for morning orders (6am-10am)

Chakula kinafika lini hasa? When exactly does food arrive?

Chakula hufika ndani ya dakika 30 kwa 95% ya maagizo yetu. Kama hakijafika ndani ya dakika 30, chakula chako ni BURE kabisa!

Food arrives within 30 minutes for 95% of our orders. If it doesn't arrive within 30 minutes, your food is completely FREE!

Dakika 30 Guarantee: 95% Hit Rate 30-Minute Guarantee: 95% Success Rate

Nawezaje kubadilisha au kufuta agizo? How can I change or cancel order?

Unaweza kubadilisha au kufuta agizo lako kwa kupiga +255 747 193 972 ndani ya dakika 10 za kwanza baada ya kupeekia. Baada ya dakika 10, tayari chakula kinaanza kutayarishwa.

You can change or cancel your order by calling +255 747 193 972 within the first 10 minutes after ordering. After 10 minutes, food preparation has already started.

Maelekezo: Piga moja kwa moja kwa mabadiliko ya haraka

Tip: Call immediately for quick changes

Wauzaji wenu wanathibitishwaje? How are your vendors verified?

Kila muuzaji anapita mchakato mkali wa uthibitishaji wa hatua 6:

Every vendor passes our rigorous 6-step verification process:

  • Ukaguzi wa usafi wa maeneo ya upishi
  • Hygiene inspection of cooking areas
  • Uthibitishaji wa ubora wa viungo
  • Ingredient quality verification
  • Ukadiriaji wa chini wa nyota 4.0 kutoka kwa wateja 100+
  • Minimum 4.0 rating from 100+ customers
  • Uthibitishaji wa uzoefu na ustadi wa upishi
  • Experience and cooking expertise verification
  • Ukaguzi wa usalama wa chakula
  • Food safety inspection
  • Uwezo wa kufikisha ndani ya dakika 30
  • Ability to deliver within 30 minutes
