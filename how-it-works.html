Frequently Asked Questions

Je, gharama ya usafirishaji ni ngapi? How much is delivery cost?

Usafirishaji ni BURE kwa maagizo yote ya TSh 10,000 na zaidi. Kwa maagizo chini ya TSh 10,000, ada ni TSh 1,500.

Delivery is FREE for all orders of TSh 10,000 and above. For orders below TSh 10,000, fee is TSh 1,500.

Chakula kinafika lini? When does food arrive?

Chakula hufika ndani ya dakika 30. Kama hakijafika ndani ya dakika 30, chakula chako ni BURE!

Food arrives within 30 minutes. If it doesn't arrive within 30 minutes, your food is FREE!

Nawezaje kulipa? How can I pay?

Unaweza kulipa kwa fedha taslimu, M-Pesa, Airtel Money, au Tigo Pesa.

You can pay with cash, M-Pesa, Airtel Money, or Tigo Pesa.

Nawezaje kubadilisha au kufuta agizo? How can I change or cancel order?

Piga +255 747 193 972 kufuta agizo lako ndani ya dakika 5 za kwanza.

Call +255 747 193 972 to cancel your order within first 5 minutes.
